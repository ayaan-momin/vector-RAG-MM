srm is a university
SRM Institute of Science and Technology is one of the top-ranking universities in India
with over 52,000 full-time students and more than 3200 faculty across all the campuses:
Kattankulathur, Ramapuram, Vadapalani Campus – all in and around Chennai,
Tiruchirappalli (in Tamil Nadu), Modinagar (in Uttar Pradesh) & Sonepat (in Haryana) – both of which are located near Delhi NCR,
Amaravati (in Andhra Pradesh), Gangtok (in Sikkim) – offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs.
